# Neuronotes

A note taking app that keeps track of your notes and tells you how much time you reviewed it.

## Features

- **Review History**: Track when you reviewed your notes, Track frequency.
- **Memory Distribution**: Using spaced repetition to categorize which information has gone into long term and short term memory.
- **Making excersises**: You can create quizzes to test your knowledge and track your progress, this can also be ai generated
- **Automatic content linking**: Your notes are automatically linked to a category
- **Depth based network analysis**: Your review history is used to analyze the category wise memory of the concepts or your proficency in one category
- **Review feed**: For recommending what information you need to learn most importantly
